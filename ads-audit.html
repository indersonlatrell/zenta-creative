The real problem

Weak results are rarely caused by one ad.

Advertising performance depends on the entire journey—from the audience and message to the page, tracking, and next action.

01

The wrong signal

Campaigns may optimise toward clicks or engagement instead of meaningful business actions.

Result: activity without clear commercial value
02

The wrong destination

Strong ads can still fail when the landing page is slow, unclear, or disconnected from the promise.

Result: paid traffic leaves without acting
03

The wrong conclusion

Incomplete tracking can make good campaigns look weak—or hide where budget is actually being wasted.

Result: decisions made from unreliable data
