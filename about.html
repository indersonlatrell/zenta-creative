Meet the founder

Hi, I’m Latrell.

I started Zenta after noticing that many businesses had websites, but very few had websites that actually helped them grow.

Businesses deserve more than a generic template or a page that looks good without doing anything useful. They need a clear digital presence that reflects their value and helps customers move from interest to action.

Every Zenta project starts with understanding the business first. We then design the messaging, experience, and technology around the real objective.

Thoughtful strategy. Intentional design. Practical growth.That is the standard behind every Zenta project.
